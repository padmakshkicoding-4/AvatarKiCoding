NOW WHAT IS GAMER OR WHO IS GAMER

A gamer is anyone who plays any game and is invested to some extent (time, money) in said game. The only difference between gamers are the genre and console of preference. And yes, Candy Crush addicts are technically gamers as well. It doesn't matter if you like it or not, a gamer is a gamer.

Dictionary.com simply states a Gamer is “a person who plays games, especially computer or video games.” Makes sense, right? By this definition, it's a very inclusive group to be a part of that seems accepting and easy going. This definition even allows Tabletop Gamers a seat at the “table”.
